The Dog House

Mary, Joshua and Rocky are three dog siblings that live together
in Lancaster, California. They live a lavish life of sleeping, running, and
playing.

Profiles

Mary

Mary is the gentle giant of the family. Being a Rottweiler mix,  
she is very hardheaded,stubborn, and big. However, she is full 
of personality and the most well trained out of the bunch.
